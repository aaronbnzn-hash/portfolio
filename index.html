Lenskart — Reimagining User Engagement

This project explores how Lenskart can increase non-transactional monthly active users (MAU) by addressing post-purchase disengagement. Using structured problem framing and RICE prioritization, I identified that while users trust the brand, they fail to see value in the app beyond eyewear transactions.

Methodology & Research

I conducted 35 survey responses and 5 in-depth interviews to uncover behavioral gaps. Core drop-offs were identified through user journey mapping of existing post-purchase flows.

Key Highlights:

  • 35 Survey Responses
  • 5 In-depth Interviews
  • RICE Prioritization
  • High-fidelity Prototyping

The Strategic Solution

I strategically selected two synergistic features to build habitual engagement:

01

Omnichannel Continuity Layer

Digitally linking offline store interactions with the app for loyalty integration and seamless record tracking.

02

Expert Eye-Care Content

Building habitual app opens through expert-led content and community participation around eye health.

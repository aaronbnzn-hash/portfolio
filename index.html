Market & User Research Summary

Key Insights

01

Engagement Decay: Eyewear is perceived as a long-term (1-2 year) purchase, leading to sharp engagement drops after delivery.

02

Expert Reassurance: 57.5% of users experience eye strain or needs between purchases but seek offline validation due to a lack of digital trust.

03

AR Try-On Friction: User dissatisfaction arises when the virtual expectations don't match the physical fit reality.

04

Retail Preference: Users strongly favor offline stores for tests due to higher perceived medical accuracy and the "human touch".

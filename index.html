Lenskart: Deep Dive

Market & User Research Findings

Core Problem

Eyewear is a low-frequency transactional category. Once a purchase is made, app engagement drops significantly, leading to high churn between buy cycles.

Reframing the Solution

Shifting perception from a one-time retailer to a continuous eye-care companion by leveraging first-party health data (prescriptions & test history).

01
Competitor Analysis

Lenskart vs Titan Eye+: Mapping value propositions, omnichannel presence, and customer experience gaps.

02
User Engagement Survey

Analyzing feedback from real users to identify post-purchase drop-off points and the demand for expert reassurance.

03
Behavioral Personas

Identifying high-value 'ideal brand advocates' vs 'offline-only users' to tailor retention notifications.
